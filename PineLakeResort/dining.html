Bistro Les Amis

Our lakefront dining room & deck provide breathtaking views of Pine Lake. Each evening, sunset is framed perfectly from your table.
Embracing the rich Pine Lake surroundings, our award-winning cuisine tastefully brings together farm fresh ingredients and local product bought daily, direct from the producer.
With emphasis placed on local & sustainable products, Chef will forage the forest for mushrooms or visit the area farms, taking great care in selecting his produce, to bring a truly distinctive experience to your plate.
