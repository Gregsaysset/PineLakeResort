Yoga by the Lake

Set within the natural tranquility, Yoga at the Pine Lake Resort offers Group classes as well as couple or private classes. Each class is an invitation to nurture your mind, body and soul. The energy of this shared experience fills an inspired space, overlooking the peaceful serenity of Pine Lake.

Biking

Pine Lake cycling loops and tours are immersed in the stunning landscape. There are lots of ways to see the scenery. There are even options to combine the shorter routes to make an epic, all-day ride. The area has some classic, and incredible, cycling experiences, like flying down to the town through Cascade Pass, or the incredible panoramic lake views.

Hiking

Pine Lake offer a variety of trails to please everyone. From a short romantic walk to an all day adventure. You will be able to fully take advantage of what Pine lake has to offer. You will surelly love the stuning lake view and beautiful Scenery

Fishing

What's best that a quiet fishing day. Simply enjoy fishing on a peacefull lake shore or take advantage of our fishing boat. We offer a wide selection of fishing accessories for the all family. Just come bring the family and enjoy Pine lake fishing.

Horseback Riding

Get to know Pine Lake on horseback. Some of the greatest advantages of touring by horse is that your horse does all of the hard work. You'll also have more opportunities to enjoy the scenic surroundings, and be able to cover a lot more ground. Pine Lake Resort Stables offers rides to suit all ability levels and interests, including sunset rides.
